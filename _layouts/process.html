OUR PROCESS

We imagined what happens if you put many intelligent and beautiful people in one room? The conversation is bound to be interesting. It will open up many vistas and make the occasion worthwhile. So we decided this will be our custom in our brainstorming from now on. And it works magic.

01
We are obsessed with questions.

That is the only way we can make our minds sharper, our products more efficient and your experience enduring. We are resistant to stress and go on till we hit the jackpot idea. It simply means getting the basics right before we start our actual journey. After this of course, there is no turning back.

02
What could go wrong?

If future can be anticipated, this is the boardroom where it happens. Our predictions from the sundry to the most disruptive innovation, goes against the principles of frugal engineering. We analyse, get our checklist right, go back to the drawing board, and re-do all the things that might go against us. This is our process of sophisticated simplicity and we relearn this process each day.

03
Time for mission critical strategy.

We avoid all the hype, the clutter and disturbance that is found in the world. Our goal is clear, aim is correct and the target is ready. But do we have enough ammunition to shoot that far? This is where we begin to perfect the art of decision making. Everything that was on paper is now translated into action.

04
Baptism by fire.

It is no doubt the most orthodox form of granting approval. But why take all the risk and put your trial by error to jeopardy? Because it makes sense to test rather than go by blind wisdom. We put the highest quality controls in the industry, set rigorous benchmarks and push it to the limits. If that can drive us to make the best, then so be it.

05
We have arrived.

Isn’t that a nice feeling to announce to the world of your achievement? Especially if you have aced a challenge that’s equivalent to scaling Mount Everest. If you have already done the latter then this is not a huge feat. But for those who of us who haven’t, this is our thirty thousand feet design treat. And yes, we did it!
